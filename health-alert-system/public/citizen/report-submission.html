Before Submitting

Please ensure that:

  • All information provided is accurate to the best of your knowledge
  • You have included specific location details
  • The description is clear and detailed
  • You are not sharing sensitive personal information about affected individuals
What to Report
Report These:
  • Suspected disease outbreaks
  • Unusual health patterns in community
  • Environmental health hazards
  • Poor sanitation conditions
  • Vector breeding sites
Don't Report:
  • Personal medical consultations
  • Emergency medical situations (call 112)
  • Non-health related issues
  • Unverified rumors
