Terms of Service

By registering with the Gombe Health Alert System, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Use the system for legitimate health reporting purposes only
  • Respect the privacy and confidentiality of health information
  • Follow guidelines set by the Gombe State Ministry of Health
  • Not misuse the system for false reporting
Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We collect and use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and maintain account security
  • Process health reports and alerts
  • Communicate important health information
  • Improve our services

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.
