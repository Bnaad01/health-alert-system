Health Worker Agreement

As a registered health worker, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate professional information
  • Use the system for legitimate health reporting and monitoring
  • Maintain patient confidentiality and privacy
  • Report genuine health concerns promptly
  • Follow professional ethics and guidelines
  • Cooperate with health authorities in emergency situations

Note: Your registration will be verified by the Gombe State Ministry of Health.
