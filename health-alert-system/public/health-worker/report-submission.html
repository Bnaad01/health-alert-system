Professional Guidelines

Please ensure:

  • Information is clinically accurate and evidence-based
  • Patient confidentiality is maintained (no identifiable information)
  • Laboratory findings are included when available
  • Case definitions follow standard protocols
  • Immediate threats to public health are clearly highlighted
Reporting Guidelines
Report Immediately:
  • Cluster of unusual symptoms
  • Suspected outbreak situations
  • Vaccine-preventable diseases
  • Severe or unusual case presentations
  • Healthcare-associated infections
Clinical Information:
  • Case definitions used
  • Laboratory confirmation status
  • Epidemiological links
  • Treatment responses
  • Public health implications
